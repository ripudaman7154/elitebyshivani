FAQs


How do I book in an appointment?
You can contact me via text on 07877065470 or fill out the form here.

Where is your salon based?
The salon is located in Heston (TW50JN) and the nearest train station is Houslow West.

How do I find you?
Theres a side black gate, you come through that into the outhouse at the back of the garden. My salon is located in there. (Gate is usually locked unless you text me to tell me you've arrived)

What is your Cancellation policy?
I require 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule appointments as I always arrange childcare and will not be able to fill that spot otherwise.
Anyone that is unable to give me that notice will be charged £5 on top of their next booking and will be required to put down a deposit for a confirmed slot. Deposits are always redeemable against bookings.

Pre Appointment Info
Nails: Please ensure anyone else's work is removed before hand as I do not touch other peoples work.
Lash Lifts: Please ensure you do not wear contact lenses and thoroughly remove eye makeup 6 hours before your appointment. Laser Hair Removal: Please ensure no oils or make up is applied to the area prior to the appointment.
Please declare any allergies BEFORE any appointment.
